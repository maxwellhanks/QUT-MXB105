# MXB105 - Calculus & Differential Equations

## Dr. Vivien Challis

### Semester 1, 2025

---

This repository provides **materials** for **MXB105 - Calculus & Differential Equations**

*The content for this unit are described below*

---

## Contents
1. Integration techniques & trigonometric substitution
2. Limits, continuity & differentiability
3. Definite integrals, Fundamental theorem of calculus, Taylor/Maclaurin polynomials
4. Taylor/Maclaurin series
5. Multivariate functions & partial derivatives
6. Directional derivatives, gradient vector, critical points for multivariate functions
7. Double & triple integrals
8. Vector-valued functions
9. First-order differential equations
10. Second-order differential equations
